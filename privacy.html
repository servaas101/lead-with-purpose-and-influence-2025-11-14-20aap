Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our Services. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our Services. They enable you to navigate the website and use essential features such as secure areas and shopping carts. Without these cookies, our Services cannot function properly.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our Services, such as which pages you visit most often and whether you receive error messages. This information helps us improve the performance and functionality of our Services.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide a more personalized experience. They may remember your login information, language preferences, or other customizations you've made.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits and are used to deliver targeted advertising based on your interests. They may be set by our advertising partners and help us understand the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to refuse cookies or be alerted when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect the functionality of our Services. You can also opt out of marketing cookies by following the opt-out links in our marketing communications.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
